About the Cloth

With Crispy Cloth I hope to open up the idea of being free and being creative. Self expression is important to ones well being and alot of times is diminished because of others opinions. The goal is to give people a push and just show them that you can do whatever it is you want as long as it makes you happy, just because someone might not understand something doesn't mean that it doesn't make sense nor that it means any less. The "Smile" tag on the back of each piece is my personal signature that symbolizes the emotion I want people to feel when they wear my clothing, I want people to smile and appreciate what they have on, in the way they wear it, no matter how they choose to style it. Love yourself and love what you do, it will get you far.
